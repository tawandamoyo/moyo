Privacy policy.

Moyo. takes a simple approach to privacy: collect only what is necessary, be transparent about it, and never sell or share visitor data. This page explains what is collected and why.

Analytics

moyo.build uses Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Plausible is a privacy-first analytics tool — it does not use cookies, does not track individuals across websites, and does not collect any personal data. Everything it measures is aggregate: page views, referral sources, and approximate country of origin.

You can read more about Plausible's approach at plausible.io/data-policy.

Email enquiries

If you send an email to hello@moyo.build, that email is stored in the email provider's infrastructure and used solely to respond to your enquiry. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing you did not request.

Cookies

moyo.build sets no cookies. None.

Third-party services

The site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google's servers receive a request that may include your IP address. This is standard browser behaviour. If you prefer to avoid it, most browsers allow you to block third-party font requests.

Your rights

If you have sent an email to hello@moyo.build and would like it deleted, send that request to the same address. It will be handled promptly and without friction.

Questions about this policy: hello@moyo.build.
