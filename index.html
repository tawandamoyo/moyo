04 About

One person.
Full accountability.

Moyo means heart. In Shona, kuita nemoyo — to do with heart — means to bring seriousness, care, and full attention to the work. Not as a slogan. As a standard.

Serious African institutions are represented online by websites that don't match the weight of their work. That gap costs them: in donor funding not received, in partnerships not formed, in professional credibility not established with the counterparties that matter. Moyo exists to close it.

Moyo. is Tawanda Moyo. When you work with Moyo., you work with me directly. I scope the project, design it, build it, and hand it over. The person who answers your email is the person who writes your code.

I take on a small number of projects at a time. Not because of capacity — because the work demands it.
