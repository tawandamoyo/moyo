# SEO Fixes Implemented - 2026-04-16

## Scope
Implemented high-impact technical and messaging fixes for search visibility and high-value conversion quality, with positioning aimed at African firms and global buyers.

## What Was Fixed

### 1) Metadata Alignment and Conversion Positioning
- Updated homepage title and description to align with premium intent:
  - "Bespoke Web & Software"
  - "African and Global Firms"
- Aligned Open Graph title/description with page intent to improve consistency across SERP snippets and shares.

Why this matters:
- Reduces intent mismatch between search snippet, social preview, and on-page message.
- Improves qualified click-through from decision-makers looking for bespoke outcomes, not commodity web work.

### 2) Social Preview Image Reliability
- Replaced missing OG image reference with an existing hosted asset:
  - `https://moyo.build/img/favicon/android-chrome-512x512.png`
- Added `twitter:*` card tags on key pages.

Why this matters:
- Prevents broken share previews.
- Improves social/link preview trust and CTR.

Note on image choice:
- The favicon 512 image is currently the most reliable available hosted image in the repository and avoids broken previews immediately. A dedicated 1200x630 OG image is still recommended as a next improvement.

### 3) Crawl and Indexation Infrastructure
- Added `robots.txt` with sitemap declaration.
- Added `sitemap.xml` containing indexable URLs.

Why this matters:
- Gives crawlers deterministic discovery paths.
- Improves indexing reliability and diagnosis in Search Console.

### 4) Analytics and Privacy Consistency
- Added Plausible script to pages (`data-domain="moyo.build"`) to match privacy policy and measurement goals.

Why this matters:
- Resolves trust gap between policy claims and implementation.
- Enables privacy-first conversion and traffic measurement.

### 5) Geographic Targeting Tone Adjustments
- Reduced overemphasis on Zimbabwe in prime conversion surfaces (hero/about/contact line).
- Kept positioning on African + global delivery.

Why this matters:
- Better matches buyer intent across pan-African and international procurement contexts.
- Preserves origin credibility without narrowing perceived operating scope.

### 6) Low-Value Page Index Control
- Added `noindex,follow` on `privacy.html` and `writing.html`.

Why this matters:
- Prevents thin or non-commercial pages from diluting index quality while still allowing link graph flow.
